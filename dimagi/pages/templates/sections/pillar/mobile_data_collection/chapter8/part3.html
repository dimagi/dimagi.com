{% blocktrans %} Incentivize the Use of Your Application {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} If the users are not given sufficient incentive to use the application, then you should expect usage to decline once the novelty of the program wears off. There are a number of ways to ensure your team continues to use your tool for the duration of your program: {% endblocktrans %}

{% include "svg/pillar/chapter3/icon1.html" %}
{# .column #}

{% blocktrans %} Save them time and effort {% endblocktrans %} {% blocktrans %} Reduction in the amount of time and effort required from a user is a great way to ensure sustained usage. If they understand that it is easier for them to fulfill their reporting requirements via the data collection app compared to using paper forms, they will be more than happy to keep using it. {% endblocktrans %}

{# .column #}
{# .grid #}
{% include "svg/pillar/chapter3/icon1.html" %}
{# .column #}

{% blocktrans %} Send them reminders {% endblocktrans %} {% blocktrans %} The implementation of reminders and nudges from supervisors into the app will help keep users on task and aware of their responsibilities. Whether you do it with a simple SMS or during weekly or monthly check-ins, it is always a good idea to let you team know what they need to do next. {% endblocktrans %}

{# .column #}
{# .grid #}
{% include "svg/pillar/chapter3/icon1.html" %}
{# .column #}

{% blocktrans %} Show them their impact {% endblocktrans %} {% blocktrans %} When users understand the impact they have, they are more likely to continue to participate. Show them the number of children they helped vaccinate or the number of institutional deliveries they carried out. {% endblocktrans %}

{# .column #}
{# .grid #}
{% include "svg/pillar/chapter3/icon1.html" %}
{# .column #}

{% blocktrans %} Give them feedback {% endblocktrans %} {% blocktrans %} Regular feedback and engagement through supervisors is another way to motivate your users. The immediate availability of a wide range of data should allow your supervisors to extract insights on the users' strengths and weaknesses. {% endblocktrans %}

{# .column #}
{# .grid #}

{% blocktrans %} To sum up, closing the feedback loop by offering tools to improve their performance will help ensure that your team continues to use your application. And the more reasons you give your users to pick up the app, the better. {% endblocktrans %}
