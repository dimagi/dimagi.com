{% blocktrans %} COVID-19 has emerged as one of the worst global pandemics in the last century. Health systems have quickly shifted resources to mitigate its toll. It is clear that the effective use of digital technology will play a core role in how effective this COVID-19 response will be. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} We are committed to doing all that we can to support the COVID-19 response, and our team brings more than a decade of experience in rapidly deploying digital solutions to complex problems. {% endblocktrans %}

To support response efforts as governments around the world look to relax social distancing guidelines, Dimagi is developing configurable applications for COVID-19 using CommCare. These applications are built for frontline workers and designed from CDC and WHO protocols to support contact tracing and case investigation. Users are able to register and track suspected cases, confirmed cases, and cases’ contacts, and follow-up for a period of required days. Additional template applications have been developed for port of entry surveillance, facility readiness, and lab test tracking. Currently, these applications are being used by governments around the world, including the city of San Francisco, the state of Alaska, and countries such as Togo, Sierra Leone, and Madagascar.
