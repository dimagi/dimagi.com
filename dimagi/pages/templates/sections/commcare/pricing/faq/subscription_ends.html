You can pause your subscription at any time. Pausing a subscription stops billing and removes access to the Applications, Reports, Data, and Messaging sections of CommCareHQ. Note that your pause will only go into effect at the end of your current payment period. Paused subscriptions can be re-upgraded to a paid subscription plan at any time. {% endblocktrans %}{% endblock %}
