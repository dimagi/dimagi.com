{% blocktrans %} Program Evaluations {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Data collected directly from a program’s beneficiaries and workers can be incredibly useful in evaluating the program’s effectiveness. CommCare can serve as a reliable platform to collect those data quickly and accurately by allowing you to employ a range of question formats (e.g. text, GPS coordinate, audio, etc.) and establish rock-solid workflows with intelligent display logic and validation conditions. These data can then be viewed and tracked in real time via integrations with a variety of data visualization tools, such as Tableau and PowerBI, or exported in CSV or Excel formats for analysis in commonly used tools such as STATA and R. {% endblocktrans %}
