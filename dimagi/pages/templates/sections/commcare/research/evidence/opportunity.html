{% blocktrans %} Longitudinal Cohort Study On Ear Biometrics For Patient ID & Tracking in Zambia {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} “We created forms in CommCare that helped us track infants longitudinally, capturing ear data every month. Based on the way we were able to set up registration, we could send out automatic reminders to patients when they were due at the clinic for their next follow-up, as well as auto-populate the correct form based on the infant’s date of birth captured on registration. Since this study was aimed at determining ear growth over the course of the first 9 months of life, we used specific follow-up forms to group our data for analysis” {% endblocktrans %}
