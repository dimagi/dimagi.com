{% blocktrans %} Catholic Relief Services {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} “CRS and partners use CommCare globally to design data collection tools for MEAL that are unique to each project – allowing staff to conduct quick one-off surveys or follow project participants over time. The online and mobile interface is easy and flexible to use for CRS and partner non-technical staff responsible for designing mobile applications and also for enumerators responsible for data collection. CommCare also improves CRS and partner digital data collection activities in areas with limited to no data connectivity, making it an ideal tool for use in both humanitarian and development contexts where connectivity might be an issue. CRS continues to expand its use of CommCare in programs globally by building a library of template applications to use and establishing communities of practice where like-minded staff can share their use of CommCare and expand their knowledge.” {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Taralyn Lyon {% endblocktrans %}
