{% blocktrans %} About {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Nearly half of organizations working with mobile applications avoid making data-informed updates to their tools because they’re afraid of the work involved. In this webinar, Amanda Springer from MiracleFeet shares MiracleFeet’s CommCare journey, and how they used data from their CommCare app to quickly review and analyze data from their 300+ clubfoot clinics around the world when the COVID-19 pandemic hit. Learn how you can inculcate a culture of data-driven decision making like MiracleFeet’s team has. {% endblocktrans %}
