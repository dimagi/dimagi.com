{% blocktrans %} The digital health interventions checklist summarizes the ways in which mobile technologies aid each aspect of an mHealth program: Clients, Healthcare Providers, Health System Managers, and Data Services. A comprehensive outline of the program – from personal health tracking to facility management – provides common understanding and alignment between parties, as well as opportunities for a standard measure of effectiveness between different digital healthcare programs. From the WHO, “this Classification framework aims to promote an accessible and bridging language for health program planners to articulate functionalities of digital health implementations.” {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Created by Dr. Garrett Mehl and Tigest Tamrat for the World Health Organization. {% endblocktrans %}
