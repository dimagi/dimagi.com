{% blocktrans %} Women and Newborns {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Problem" %}: {% blocktrans %} In many countries, women are required to keep track of their own paper medical records, which can be lost or damaged. In many cases, if a woman forgets or loses her medical records the facility staff will refuse to see her or her baby. Additionally, women may lose track of time between ANC or PNC appointments. {% endblocktrans %}

  • {% blocktrans %} Mobile applications eliminate the need for paper medical records by storing medical information for each woman and newborn registered through the application. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %} Case management allows women and newborns to rely on their CHW to recall the details of their medical histories. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %} SMS reminders enable women to be notified about upcoming appointments without requiring CHW prompting. {% endblocktrans %}
