{% blocktrans %} In West Africa, CommCare is being used to improve core infectious disease response roles in the Ebola epidemic and in future disease outbreaks {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} These areas include adherence to screening and triage protocols, sensitization and information dissemination, surveillance and contact tracing, diagnostics and lab tracking, stock tracking, and patient self-reporting. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Even as affected countries move beyond the Ebola epidemic, CommCare enables implemented programs to shift focus to other health and development areas. These areas include livelihoods, infectious disease surveillance, maternal and child health, diagnostics and lab tracking, and core responsibilities of frontline workers that are supported by CommCare. {% endblocktrans %}
