{% blocktrans %} Health Workers {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Problem" %}: {% blocktrans %} CHWs undergo extensive trainings, which require them to retain large amounts of information, which is frequently forgotten after a short time. Additionally, CHWs often have to track their own patients and submit that data to managers with little organizational support. This process often leads to lack of follow-up with patients and inadequate data collection systems. {% endblocktrans %}

  • {% blocktrans %} Training manuals and behavior change communication materials are integrated within the app on a CHW’s phone, ensuring easy access to accurate information. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %} Multimedia allows pregnant women and mothers to listen to messages in their own language or view culturally relevant visuals alongside their CHW. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %} Real time submission of data directly from the CHW’s phone enables managers and supervisors to access data on health outcomes and CHW performance with up to date records. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %} Case management gives CHWs autonomy to manage their own case list directly through the mobile application, rather than relying on their memories or paper records systems when it comes to reaching patients. {% endblocktrans %}
