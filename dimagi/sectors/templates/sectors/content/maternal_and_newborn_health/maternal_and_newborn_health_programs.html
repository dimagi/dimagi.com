{% blocktrans %} Maternal and Newborn Health Programs {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Problem" %}: {% blocktrans %} Many maternal and newborn health programs are built on a foundation that relies heavily on access to clinics or hospitals, particularly when it comes to referrals for high-risk patients, burdening CHWs who often encounter difficulties in identifying emergency symptoms and working within inadequate and complex referral systems. {% endblocktrans %}

  • {% blocktrans %} Forms enable CHWs to use standardized, complex workflows to identify symptoms in mothers and newborns that require emergency care. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %} Case sharing enables CHWs to immediately send medical information directly to clinicians at the referral facility and improve lines of communication between field and clinical work. {% endblocktrans %}
