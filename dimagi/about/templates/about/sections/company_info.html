{% blocktrans %} How we got our start {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Dimagi was founded in 2002 out of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology Media Lab. While volunteering for an electrical engineering class, Jonathan Jackson, co-founder and CEO, met Dr. Vikram Kumar, co-founder and chief medical officer, who was completing his masters thesis at the MIT Media Lab and working on an early version of Dimagi. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Kumar wanted to build a company that combined global health and technology, but had not yet decided how. With Jacksonâ€™s help, the two focused on developing software for low-resource settings and started building among the first mobile solutions for frontline health programs in developing countries. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Today, Dimagi continues to operate on the foundation that mobile solutions are key to improving the quality and efficiency of global development programs. While we got our start in health care, we work across sectors today with organizations of all sizes. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Dimagi is a recognized social enterprise and certified Benefit Corporation, reflecting our commitment to making an impact. {% endblocktrans %}
