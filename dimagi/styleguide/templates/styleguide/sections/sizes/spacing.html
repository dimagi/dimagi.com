Spacing

There are six different types of white space options.

  • none 0 px
  • base 10 px (scss variable: $space-base)
  • line 20 px (scss variable: $space-line)
  • small 30 px (scss variable: $space-small)
  • medium 60 px (scss variable: $space-small)
  • large 80 px
